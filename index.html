About the Summit

Asia's Most Influential Energy Gathering

The Asia Future Energy Summit (AFES) is the region's foremost conference dedicated to accelerating the energy transition. Now in its 8th edition, AFES brings together utilities, regulators, technology providers, financiers, and innovators to tackle the most pressing challenges reshaping Asia-Pacific's energy landscape.


From decarbonisation mandates to grid modernisation and energy security, AFES delivers actionable intelligence, strategic connections, and real-world case studies that drive decisions — not just conversations.

Focus Areas

Key Themes & Tracks

Six powerful tracks. One strategic agenda. Dive deep into the topics shaping the future of energy across Asia-Pacific.

🌱
Decarbonisation & Net Zero
Pathways to carbon neutrality, emissions trading mechanisms, and cross-sector collaboration for achieving net-zero targets by 2050.
⚡
Renewables & Storage
Solar, wind, hydrogen, and large-scale battery storage — financing, deployment strategies, and grid integration at scale.
🔌
Grid Modernisation
Smart grids, demand response, microgrids, and distributed energy resources transforming legacy infrastructure.
💡
Digitalisation & AI
AI-driven energy management, digital twins, predictive analytics, and data-driven grid optimisation shaping the utility of the future.
💰
Energy Finance & Investment
Green bonds, blended finance, ESG criteria, and the capital flows accelerating Asia's clean energy transition.
🛡️
Energy Security & Policy
Geopolitical dynamics, regulatory frameworks, supply chain resilience, and regional energy cooperation strategies.
